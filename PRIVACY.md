# Privacy Policy for Suno Downloader Mrarranger

Last Updated: January 2025

## Overview

Suno Downloader Mrarranger ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following types of data:

### Website Content

- Song titles, audio URLs, and metadata from Suno.com pages you visit

- - This data is used solely to enable the download functionality
 
  - ### User Preferences
 
  - - Your preferred download format (MP3 or WAV)
   
    - - Download settings stored locally in your browser
     
      - ## How We Use Your Information
     
      - We use the collected information exclusively to:
     
      - - Display available songs for download
       
        - - Enable single and bulk download functionality
         
          - - Save your preferences for future use
           
            - ## Data Storage
           
            - - All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
             
              - - We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
               
                - - We do NOT collect personal information
                 
                  - - We do NOT track your browsing history
                   
                    - ## Third-Party Services
                   
                    - The Extension only interacts with Suno.com to retrieve song information. We do not share any data with third parties.
                   
                    - ## Data Security
                   
                    - Your data remains on your local device. We do not have access to any information stored by the Extension.
