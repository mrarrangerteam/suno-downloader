# Privacy Policy for Suno Workspace Manager (My Suno Exporter)

Last Updated: January 2025

## Overview

Suno Workspace Manager ("the Extension") is a personal productivity tool designed to help you manage and export **your own songs** that you created in your Suno.com workspace. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

**Important:** This extension only works with songs in your personal Suno workspace that you created yourself. It does not access or download songs created by other users.

## Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following types of data:

### Website Content
- Song titles, audio URLs, and metadata **only from your personal Suno.com workspace**
-   - This data is used solely to enable the download functionality for your own creations
    -   - We only access songs that belong to your authenticated account
     
        - ### User Preferences
        - - Your preferred download format (MP3 or WAV)
          - - Download settings stored locally in your browser
           
            - ## How We Use Your Information
           
            - We use the collected information exclusively to:
           
            - - Display your own songs available for download from your workspace
              - - Enable single and bulk export functionality for your personal creations
                - - Save your preferences for future use
                 
                  - ## Data Storage
                 
                  - - All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
                    - - We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
                      - - We do NOT collect personal information
                        - - We do NOT track your browsing history
                          - - We do NOT access songs from other users' accounts
                           
                            - ## Third-Party Services
                           
                            - The Extension only interacts with Suno.com to retrieve information about **your own songs** from your authenticated workspace. We do not share any data with third parties.
                           
                            - ## Data Security
                           
                            - Your data remains on your local device. We do not have access to any information stored by the Extension.
                           
                            - ## Scope of Access
                           
                            - This extension:
                            - - ✅ Only accesses your personal Suno workspace
                              - - ✅ Only downloads songs you created yourself
                                - - ✅ Requires your Suno.com authentication
                                  - - ❌ Does NOT access public song libraries
                                    - - ❌ Does NOT download other users' songs
                                      - - ❌ Does NOT bypass any copyright protections
